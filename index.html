About Me

I grew up in a small town of North Carolina, graduated high school and went to college like you're supposed to. Being young and naieve I overlooked the true value of education and performed very poorly in school and ended up dropping out shortly after enrolling. After leaving school I worked part time from job to job until I found a good spot working as an automotive mechanic. While the pay was good, everything else was awful. Long hours, harsh working conditions and the constant strain on my body left me looking for something better. One day while looking online for potential job openings I stumbled upon a UNC course for web development and decided to enroll.
